Robotics

A robot is a mobile machine equipped with a comupter that helps to perform actions previously programmed

The Basics of Robotics

  1. Mechanical construction
  2. Electric Element
  3. Software

Types of robots

Medical robot Sports robot Home robot

Robotics features

  • Protecting people from exposure to dangers such as searching for mines and exposure to harmful rays.
  • A person is not bored with repetitive actions.
  • Speed and accuracy in work, thus increasing production, and clearly reducing expenses.
    • Overcoming the problem of shortage of manpower.

Robotics disadvantages

  • It poses a security risk, because it controls information remotely, which could be videos, recorded voices, and dangerous and sensitive information.
  • Unable to grasp a certain random part without using a special vision system.
  • It increases the unemployment problem.
  • It causes many technical problems, especially in the field of artificial intelligence and machine vision.
